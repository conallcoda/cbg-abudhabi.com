Description: <p>As blockchain infrastructure scales and tokenized systems go live, the surface area for attacks and compliance risk grows in tandem. With high-profile hacks and rising regulatory pressure, the next wave of innovation must prioritize safeguarding users, assets, and data. From zero-knowledge proofs and modular architectures to smart contract security and embedded privacy, the focus is shifting toward building resilience. How can current and next-generation blockchain infrastructure meet this demand?</p>
