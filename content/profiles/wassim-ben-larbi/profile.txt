Bio:

Mohamed Wassim Ben Larbi is an anchor, journalist and producer. 
He studied Finance in Toulouse University in France. 
Has been working for TV and Radio since 2009. Hosted 3 different TV shows on the Tunisian National TV. He started his first radio programm on the International Tunisian Radio Channel. Currently hosting the morning show in radio Express FM. He is doing major Business and Political interviews.  
He moderates major panels and hosts main ceremonies on Business, technology economic topics etc ... 
Awarded the prestigious Academia Prize in Tunisia and has been chosen among the 100 people moving the Business in Tunisia three years on a row. Selected as one of the 100 Young Leaders for Tunisia. Elected as Best radio Host in Tunisia two years on a row.
