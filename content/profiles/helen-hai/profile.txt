Bio: Helen Hai is head of Binance NFT, Binance Charity and a UNIDO Goodwill Ambassador. She was named a 2015 Young Global Leader by World Economic Forum, received the 2015 African Business Icon Award, and was awarded as Officer of the National Order of Lion of Senegal in 2016. Named 2017 Wu Fellow by UC Berkeley, Global Visionary by UBS and 2018 Aspen Fellow by Aspen Institute, Helen holds a BA and MSc degree from University of London, and EMBA from INSEAD and Tsinghua University.
