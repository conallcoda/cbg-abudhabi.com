Bio: Tobias is the initiator of the Crypto Finance Conference. He is also Chairman and Co-Founder of Crypto Finance AG. 10 years ago, he founded SUSI Partners AG, a EUR 1.1 bn Swiss infrastructure fund manager. Tobias has completed the Singularity University Executive Program in 2016 and holds a Phd in Business Administration from the European Business School. As an investor, Tobias has invested in various start-ups in the blockchain, virtual reality and e-health sector.
