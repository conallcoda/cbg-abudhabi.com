Bio:

Eric Wragge is Global Head of Business Development & Capital Markets at the Algorand Foundation.  Previously, he was employed by J.P. Morgan in various Structured Finance and Solutions roles, ultimately serving in its Securitised Products Group as Managing Director.  
 
He based in London and is a member of the Investment Advisory Board for the U.N. Office of Disaster Risk Reduction, is an advisory Board Member of Pan American Finance, and is co-founder of Rochester Square, a socially engaged project transforming a formerly derelict inner city garden nursery into a thriving hub for the arts.
