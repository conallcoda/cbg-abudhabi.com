Bio: Konstantin is the CEO & Founder of Blockdaemon, the leading infrastructure tool for the blockchain space. Konstantin graduated from the European Business School, before starting to build commercially viable networks at Deutsche Telekom, T-Mobile & Nokia. He build a few companies monetizing connecting data-silos, when he discovered bitcoin and blockchain and its connection between network-effect and value-creation. Konstantin serves on the Board of Directors of Odyssey House, the largest NYC based Housing & Treatment Centre for the marginalized, the mentally ill and homeless.
