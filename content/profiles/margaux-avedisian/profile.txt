Bio:

Featured in the WSJ, New York based Margaux Avedisian is an investor, entrepreneur, publicist and advisor in the Bitcoin and Blockchain industry since 2012.

Margaux is currently CMO at Alphabit Fund, which has 650% total return since 2017. Previously she was EVP at Transform PR, the leading blockchain public relations firm which has represented more than 100 blockchain companies and over 60 ICOs including Ethereum, Augur, Golem, Maidsafe, Wax, Gnosis, Bancor and Unikoin. 

Prior to that, Margaux cofounded MonetaGo (a blockchain value transfer company), and Tradehill (the first Bitcoin exchange in America) as well as AlphaPoint (a white label exchange software solution). She was featured in the documentary “The Rise and Rise of Bitcoin” and is a standup comedian.
