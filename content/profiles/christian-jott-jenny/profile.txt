Bio: Christian Jott Jenny (born in 1978) is a classical tenor, actor and founder of the Amt für Ideen. He studied classical singing and acting in Berlin and has performed in operas, operettas, musical theater and recitals in Switzerland and Germany. Christian established his artistic alter ego in the character Leo Wundergut. Wundergut & his Jet-Set-Singers have performed their program, Wundergut & Jet-Set-Singers both at home and abroad. In 1997, Jenny founded the Amt für Ideen. This 'Office of Ideas' connects culture with businesses, private individuals, institutions and products. Christian is the founder and curator of Festival da Jazz St. Mortiz.
