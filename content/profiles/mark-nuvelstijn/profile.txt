Bio: Mark Nuvelstijn is Co-founder and CEO of the Dutch digital currency exchange platform Bitvavo. Mark completed two bachelor's degrees in Groningen: Business Administration and Law with a master's degree in Tax Law / Tax Law. After completing his studies, he worked for Loyens & Loeff as a Tax Lawyer. In 2018 he started Bitvavo together with two other founders. The aim of Bitvavo is to make the new economy of digital currencies accessible.
