Bio: Brian is a global entrepreneur in blockchain, mobile, internet-based software and healthy lifestyles service businesses in the U.S. and Asia. He has raised hundreds of millions of dollars in private equity funding from global investors and led the purchase and sale of several global enterprises. He is also a co-founder, board member and managing partner of Pacific Springboard. Previously, Brian co-founded Moxie Software and iMark.com, which was acquired for $340 million by a publicly-traded enterprise software company that was later acquired by SAP. Brian is a passionate advocate of inclusion awareness, advocacy and education. He is a board member at Trinity Episcopal School of Austin and The Rise School of Austin and is a member of the Entrepreneurship at Cornell Advisory Council. Brian holds an MBA from Harvard Business School.
