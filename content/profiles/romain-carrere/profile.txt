Bio:

Prior to joining Aura Blockchain Consortium, Romain Carrere dedicated his effort to advising web3 start-ups on product launches and on scaling their operations. He also provided advisory services to corporations interested in venturing into blockchain, NFT and metaverse projects. His past experiences include founding a digital agency, building e-commerce pure players and advising a number of luxury brands in the past on their digital strategies. 

Romain Carrere has a proven track record of building and scaling businesses, with over 15 years of diverse and relevant experience as a tech entrepreneur. During that time, he spent time in leadership roles in five start-ups, with three successful exits, four years in content production, and two years in Investment Banking.  

Romain Carrere holds a Master’s degree from ESCP and graduated cum laude with a Bachelor’s degree from Bentley University. He has actively participated in various delegations, including representing French entrepreneurs at the G20 delegation in Tokyo.
