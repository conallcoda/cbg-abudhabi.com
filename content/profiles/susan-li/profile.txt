Bio:

Award-winning globally-experienced journalist who has anchored programs on all 3 major continents including financial capitals New York, London and Hong Kong.

Susan has now worked at all 3 of the world’s major financial networks: Fox Business, CNBC & Bloomberg TV. Currently, Susan helps lead coverage on business and the economy on Fox Business & Fox News which is America’s most watched cable news channel for more than 20 years. Previous to Fox, she hosted CNBC’s Worldwide Exchange, Asia Squawkbox & started CNBC’s luxury travel show First Class.  At Bloomberg TV, Susan was the main anchor in the Asia Pacific with her namesake show First Up with Susan Li awarded as the Asia Pacific’s Best News Program in 2012 while Susan was highly commended as the Asia Pacific’s Best Anchor. Throughout her career, Susan has interviewed the world’s leading newsmakers and policy- setters including Apple’s CEO Tim Cook, Canada’s Prime Minister Justin Trudeau & Japan’s former Prime Minister Shinzo Abe. As a global citizen, Susan is passionate about telling stories that affect all communities.
