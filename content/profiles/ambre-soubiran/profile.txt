Bio: Ambre Soubiran is the CEO and Chairman of Kaiko, the leading independent provider of cryptocurrency market data and analytics. With two decades of experience in finance, she has raised over $80M in venture funding and expanded Kaiko globally. Previously, she worked at HSBC Global Markets in London, specializing in equity derivatives. A Paris Dauphine University graduate with a master's in applied mathematics and computer science, Ambre has been involved in cryptocurrencies since 2012, combining her technical background with financial innovation.
