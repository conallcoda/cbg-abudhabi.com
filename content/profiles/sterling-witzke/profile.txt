Bio:

Sterling Witzke is an investor and entrepreneur who is passionate about startups and helping founders change the world. Most recently, she was a partner at Winklevoss Capital where she lead investments spanning multiple verticals and asset classes for over seven years. Sterling continues to provide ongoing strategic support to Winklevoss Capital’s portfolio of startups in her current role as a Senior Advisor.

Prior to Winklevoss Capital, Sterling was on the founding team of Turo (formerly RelayRides), the nation’s first peer-to-peer car sharing service, and served as its Chief Business Development Officer. She holds a Bachelor of Arts with Honors from Stanford University and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.

Sterling is also an advisor to TitleTown Tech (the investment arm of the Green Bay Packers), a member of the Venture Board of the Kairos Society, and a member of the Advisory Board of the Crypto Finance Conference. In her spare time, Sterling volunteers for the Humane Society and the American Cancer Society.
