Bio:

Steve Swain is Co-founder & CEO of Lendingblock, an open exchange for cryptocurrency loans, where institutional borrowers and lenders are instantly matched in a simple, secure and transparent way. Lendingblock is introducing the concept of securities lending to the crypto economy.

Steve Swain is an experienced financial services technology professional, with outstanding record built during 15 years hands-on management roles with top-tier global banks, including Lehman, Credit Suisse, Macquarie and UBS, followed by 5 years as a big-4 technology consulting partner focused on financial services. He has spent his time working advising institutions on regulatory technology and fintech in London, New York and Sydney. Steve has a degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from University of Adelaide.
