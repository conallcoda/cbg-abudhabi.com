Bio: Marc Bettinger is currently a Director of Forctis AG, a Swiss-based company created to disrupt the cryptocurrency space through a completely new blockchain technology. Starting his banking career 20 years ago in Germany, from his early days he’s had a faible for alternative & value investments. Marc is a Certified International Investment Analyst (CIIA) and until recently worked for a leading Swiss Private Bank as Credit Risk Manager. From 2013-2017 he was active as a crypto-blogger under the pseudonym “Altcoinspekulant” building one of the pioneer blogs regarding Altcoin-investing, with an incredible track record during his time in charge. In addition, he is Co-Host of the Altcoin-Meetup Switzerland and initiated the Social Media group “Altcoins Switzerland.” He is married and has three young boys.
