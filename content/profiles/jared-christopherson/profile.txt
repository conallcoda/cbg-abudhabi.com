Bio:

Jared is an award-winning digital performance marketer and data expert with 15+ years experience in the advertising industry. He is the co-founder of YellowHammer Media Group, which counts Casper, Campbell's, Warby Parker, LivingSocial, and Postmates as past clients, generating upwards of $100M annual advertising revenue.  He is also the co-founder of SpringServe, an open video ad serving platform for brands.  

After exiting the centralized advertising world, Jared co-founded TAP, a decentralized Blockchain advertising platform for Fortune 500 brands. TAP allows brands to manage millions of micro-campaigns and relationships directly with consumers on a 1-to-1 basis, with measurable performance results.
