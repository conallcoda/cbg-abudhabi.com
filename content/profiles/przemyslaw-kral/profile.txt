Bio: Przemysław Kral is the CEO of zondacrypto and serves on its board of directors. With a Master's in Law from the University of Silesia, he has a long history of working as an advocate and extensive experience in management. In his tenure as CEO at zondacrypto, Przemyslaw Kral is successfully spearheading the company's global expansion. With in-depth knowledge of cryptocurrencies and extensive experience in the implementation of KYC and AML procedures, Kral was instrumental in securing zondacrypto’s Estonian FIU license and further regulatory approvals.
