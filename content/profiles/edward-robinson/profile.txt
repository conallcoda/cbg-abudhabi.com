Bio: Edward Robinson is an award-winning editor and writer with 25 years of experience in financial and business journalism. As a senior writer at Bloomberg News, Edward wrote about banking, venture capital, hedge funds, ESG, and Brexit. He was also regular commentator on Bloomberg Television. In 2015, he co-wrote a cover story for Bloomberg Markets magazine on Blythe Masters' embrace of blockchain technology, which introduced cryptocurrencies to many traditional finance professionals. Edward is now a contributing editor at The Defiant, and writes a column on cryptocurrencies for Institutional Investor.
