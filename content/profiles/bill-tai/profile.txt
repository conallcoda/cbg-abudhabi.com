Bio:

Bill Tai has been funding startups as a VC since 1991 and has had over 20 seed and early-stage projects grow to become publicly listed companies.  He is Zoom Video’s 1st backer and among the first backers of ‘unicorns’ Canva, Color Genomics, Class.com, Dapper Labs, and SafetyCulture.  

Involved in the Bitcoin ecosystem since 2010, he is an early backer of Bitfury Group having personally funded their Bitcoin mining ASIC ~8 years ago. He is Board Chair of one of North America's largest publicly listed Bitcoin and Ethereum mining operations, HUT8 Mining (NASDAQ:HUT), an Angel backer of Dapper Labs (creator of Cryptokitties and NBA Topshot), and a cofounder of NFT companies MetaGood.com and OneOf.com. 

He has an operating background as co-founding Chairman of data science pioneer Treasure Data (acq. by SOFTBANK), founding Chairman of network operating system pioneer IPInfusion (Tokyo Stock Exchange:4813) and Founding CEO and Chair of iAsiaWorks (IPO Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley), which operated Internet Data Centers in 10 countries across Asia in the 1990's. 

Originally trained as a computer chip designer, he joined the CEO of Fairchild Semiconductor at the startup of LSI Logic during the ‘siliconization’ of Silicon Valley and was issued Badge #A001 during the formative years of Taiwan Semiconductor (NYSE TSM) which is now among the ten most valuable companies in the world. He started the semiconductor practice at Alex Brown & Sons in the 1980's, handling the IPO’s of companies such as Atmel, Cirrus Logic, and Zilog. 

He holds a BSEE with honors from the University of Illinois, an MBA from Harvard and Chairs ACTAI Global, a 501c3 uniting Athletes, Conservationists, Technologists, Artists, and Innovators to fuel technology-based environmental conservation and economic development via entrepreneurship.  He is among the catalytic advisors to giga.global, bringing broadband connectivity to schools around the world.
