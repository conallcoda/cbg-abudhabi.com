Bio:

Florian, Managing Partner at Bamboo Capital Partners, entrepreneur and impact investor, holds a BBA and Master of Science in Marketing and leverages his extensive background on catalytic capital, technology, fundraising and personal development to help impact investing in and building meaningful organizations tackling the world’s biggest challenges. His background includes success in leading business transformations and raising hundreds of millions of dollars for funds and companies.
In particular, he is passionate about technologies leapfrogging poverty, impact investment and effective personal development methodologies to create high-performance organizations. Florian is Founding Chair of the Planet Action Network at the Young Presidents’ Organization (www.ypo.org) and has held executive positions at Parter Capital, Olympus, SBi (Stryker) and B.Braun. Fluent in German, English, Spanish, French & Italian.
