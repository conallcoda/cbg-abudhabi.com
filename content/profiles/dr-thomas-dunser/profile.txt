Bio: Thomas is a senior consultant to the prime minister of Liechtenstein. He is responsible for innovation and digitalization topics within the ministry of finance and was the project leader of the “Blockchain-Act” in Liechtenstein. Prior to joining the ministry of public affairs and finance he was working in the financial sector as entrepreneur and head of group asset & liability management at VP Bank in Vaduz. He holds a PhD in engineering of the Swiss Federal Institute in Zurich.
