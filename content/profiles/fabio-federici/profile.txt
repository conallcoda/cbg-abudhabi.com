Bio:

Mr. Fabio Federici is Founder & CEO at Base58 Capital, an asset & hedge fund management firm specializing in crypto assets. Previously he spent three years in Silicon Valley as co-founder and CEO of Skry, Inc. - a cryptocurrency and blockchain analytics software company acquired by Bloq, Inc. in February 2017.

Before founding Skry Fabio was enrolled at the Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Art in Switzerland, pursuing a Master of Business Administration. One semester into the program Mr. Federici got handed the opportunity to take Skry and join the acclaimed 500 Startups accelerator program in Mountain View, California.

Before the acquisition in 2017, Skry had raised over $1 million in venture capital from Silicon Valley, obtained various nominations like “Top Technology to Watch 2014” from the Government Technology Research Alliance and participated in the finals of the BBVA “Open Talent” Competition, as well as the UBS “Future of Finance Challenge” in 2015.
