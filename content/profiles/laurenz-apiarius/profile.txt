Bio:

Founder & Managing Partner of Blockwall, the first regulatory and legally compliant German crypto asset fund focused on investments in Distributed Ledger/Blockchain technologies serving Institutional Investors, Family Offices and UHNWIs to gain access to this asset class.

He is also non-executive director in two family businesses, which are a high-performance polymer producer and a manufacturer for rheological testing devices. Previous active & passive engagements in various PE and VC investments.

Educated in Germany (EBS University), South Africa (University of Stellenbosch) and Scotland (University of St. Andrews). Member of EO.
