Bio: Richard Schäli is a Swiss investor, entrepreneur and high school student. At seven, he purchased his first stock, selling it a year later for a 20% profit. By ten, he secured first place in a global trading competition with 2.7 million participants. At eleven, he founded the Richifinancial Trading Group to manage assets for friends, later converting this fund into a publicly traded certificate. Today, as the head of Secanta Capital, Schäli advises on eight-figure portfolios for Ultra-High-Net-Worth Individuals, focusing on long-term investments in technology companies. Alongside his professional endeavors, he is a high school student from Zug, Switzerland and spent six months in Singapore learning Mandarin and broadening his horizon. In 2023, he initiated Camp Corvatsch, a gathering for renowned entrepreneurs and young founders. Schäli has been named to the Forbes 30 Under 30 list for the DACH region and is recognized as one of Switzerland’s youngest financial advisors.
