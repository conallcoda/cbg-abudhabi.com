Bio:

Yuri is CEO and Co-Founder of IPStock, a company based in Switzerland and Singapore since 2015 and going ICO in 2018. IPStock implements visual digital content registry on blockchain and license management through smart-contracts. Yuri is also a Senior partner of FP Wealth Solutions, a Swiss multi-family office. Yuri has an extensive work experience with stock market, private equity and venture investments.

Previously he worked in Fleming Family & Partners, Russian Nanotechnologies Corporation (RUSNANO) and Renaissance Capital. Yuri graduated with honours from the Law Faculty and did postgraduate research in Saint Petersburg State University of Aerospace Instrumentation, Russia.
