Bio:

Serial entrepreneur & business executive with substantial practical experience in the
start-up, technology & social networking development sectors. He has a strong
background in developing products, business strategy and raising venture capital.

He introduced the first tokenized crypto index fund - CRYPTO20 and is the founder and managing director of the world's first licensed Bitcoin sportsbook at BetVIP. He is the CEO & Founder of free telecoms start-up FSMS.co.za with over 325 000 registered users and 25 million free SMSes delivered to 3.5 million unique recipients

He holds a honours degree in chemical engineering through the University of Cape
Town.
