Bio:

Paul Savchuk (born March 9th, 1992) is an American young entrepreneur, investor, trader who is originally from Ukraine. He was born and raised in Kiev. Savchuk is the co-founder of Cryptocurrency Capital LLC American based hedge fund.

Savchuk does not consider himself a member of any school of economic thought, but he believes in full decentralization and new economic blockchain revolution.
From 2009 to 2013 while earning bachelor’s degree, his career background includes managing media production, involvement in IT business specialized in processing bank transfers, managing portfolio of stocks (CFDs), trading currencies on FX.

In 2013, Savchuk graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Economics from National Aviation University (Kiev, Ukraine). He started to work as a foreign trade manager for Gresa Group, the company specialized in renewable energy and biofuels. In 2015, he acquired master's degree in Business Intelligence from the same school and in 2017 he took an MBA degree from Cambridge College (Boston).

In March 2017, Savchuk joined T3 Trading Group, where he was trading securities.

He became interested in cryptocurrencies in 2016. However, he performed trades only for his personal account. Since May 13th of 2017, Paul has started deep research about cryptocurrencies bubbles and how governments deal with crypto markets.

He realized that modern regulations have multiple gaps that create additional chaos for crypto markets. Thus, his main goal as a hedge fund manager in cooperation with Tymofii Melnyk to create a safe harbor for American and Foreign investors. Partners of Cryptocurrency Capital are focused on:

providing deep analysis to investors

pooled funds management, also by eliminating influence of bubble risks

creating safe harbor, for those who have lost money after investing in fake ICOs
