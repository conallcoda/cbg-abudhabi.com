Bio:

Andy Qi GE, is the Managing Partner at Diligence Capital, a private asset manager with primary business as fund of PE/VC funds investments. Andy also serves as Vice Chairman of US-China M&A Association, as well as board members of several investment companies.
Prior to joining Diligence Capital, Andy worked for Partners Group, a global asset management company where he was in charge of fund structuring and investment solutions in China. Earlier in his career he worked for the Legislative Affairs Office of the State Council of the PRC, where he participated in drafting more than 30 legislations in the fields of finance, capital markets, investments, industry policy, SOE- regulation and PE/VC.
Andy holds a Bachelor's degree of Laws from China Foreign Affairs University and a Master's degree of Laws in Banking and Finance from University College London. Andy is awarded the Top 10 Most Market- Oriented FOF manager in 2018-2019 by the China Limited Partners Association (CLPA).
