Bio: Viktor is the Managing Partner of Rockaway Blockchain Fund. Prior to Rockaway, Viktor worked at McKinsey & Co focusing on corporate finance and M&A. Before that, he co-founded Innovatrics, a start-up focused on biometric identification, which won multiple awards, such as Deloitte Top 50 growth companies. As a hobby, Viktor has been teaching corporate finance and valuations at Charles University in Prague, University Paris XI, and startup accelerators. Viktor holds a Masters degree from HEC Paris and MBA from INSEAD.
