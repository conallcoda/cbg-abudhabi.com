Bio:

Brigitte Luginbühl is the CEO and founder of Crypto Real Estate AG which is launching the first professional real estate backed crypto-currency. Backed by Swiss real estate and compliant with regulations, the SwissRealCoin becomes a securitized and more stable crypto-currency, and eventually the world’s most important safe haven currency.

Brigitte Luginbühl is an experienced real estate professional with more than 10 years proven track record in Real Estate Corporate Finance. She has been with the global market leader Jones Lang LaSalle in Switzerland and London. She managed numerous large-scale and complex real estate transactions with a volume of >CHF 1bn in total and was project leader of many valuation projects including recurring large portfolio valuations with a volume of >CHF 9bn annually. She is the youngest female member of Efficiency Club.
