Bio:

Zenobia Godschalk is the founder and CEO of ZAG Communications, a digital marketing, PR, and IR firm that has launched and scaled multi-billion dollar enterprise tech companies. 
Zenobia was on the founding team of Hedera, and served as SVP of Communications from 2018-2024. Previously, Zenobia was a sell-side equity research analyst at Morgan Keegan, covering Internet Security and Infrastructure markets. She was awarded the Wall Street Journal's #1 Software Analyst Team honor in 2006. She joined Morgan Keegan from Opsware (NASDAQ: OPSW), where she was part of the founding team, ran communications, and helped grow the business to $100M in revenue, before its acquisition by HP for $1.6B. 
She is a board member at Stanford FLAN (First Gen/Low Income Alumni Network), The Trinity School, Acoer, and Instarails.
