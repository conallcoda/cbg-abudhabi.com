Bio: Rebecca Rettig is General Counsel at the Aave companies, a group of software development company.  She began her legal career at Cravath, Swaine & Moore LLP in New York; prior to joining Aave, Rebecca was a partner at a large U.S. law firm, advising companies in the blockchain and crypto space for the past 4.5 years—with a particular focus advising DeFi projects over the past two years.  She provided regulatory advice as well as represented clients in all aspects of litigation and regulatory enforcement proceedings .  In 2019, Rebecca was recognized as one of the top 100 women lawyers in New York City by Crain’s New York Business.
