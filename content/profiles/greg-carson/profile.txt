Bio:

Greg is Managing Partner at XBTO Humla Ventures, and head of corporate Development and Ventures for XBTO Group.
Greg helps world class entrepreneurs and CEOs grow their startups competitively with capital and mentoring. He has spent his life working with entrepreneurs to improve the future. Greg has a decade as a tech founder, a decade of investment banking and strategy, and is closing out his first decade of venture capital.

He is a frequent contributor, lecturer and speaker on podcasts and events about Bitcoin and digital assets, negotiation technique and growth. He has spoken on these subjects at several global conferences including Bitcoin Miami, Consensus Invest, MCC, the Crypto Finance Conference, Davos, Horizon 2020 events, and even at his Alma Mater, the Wharton school of business.
