Bio:

Aurora Schwarz is Director of Strategic Partnerships at Wintermute, one of the largest liquidity providers globally for digital assets. Prior to joining Wintermute, Aurora was Vice President at Citi Private Bank delivering cross-asset advisory in Fixed Income, FX, Equities and Derivatives, providing tactical trading ideas and execution to ultra-high net worth (UHNW) and sophisticated family offices and served on the Citi Private Bank Growth Board for new innovation and technology.

Aurora has an entrepreneurial background, she founded two start-ups focused on luxury consumer goods and ecommerce.
