Bio:

Howard Morgan is Chair and General Partner at B Capital and a pioneer of early-stage investing. He co-founded First Round Capital, helped found Idealab and served as the Founding President of Renaissance Technologies.
He has served as a professor at the University of Pennsylvania and Cornell University and as an Executive Fellow at UC Berkeley. 
Howard currently serves as Director of Idealab and on the boards of Cornell University, NY Public Library, Cold Spring Harbor Laboratories and Math for America. 
He has a PhD from Cornell University and a BS from City College of the City University of New York.
