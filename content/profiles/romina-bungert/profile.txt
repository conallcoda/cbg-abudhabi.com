Bio:

Romina Bungert is COO at DSTOQ, a licensed securities exchange that enables cross-border and peer-to-peer trading of tokenized securities. Romina specializes on regulatory matters related to blockchain and fintech topics and has been co-creating several ventures in this field.

Prior to DSTOQ, she has supported several blockchain companies token engineering and has been the first employee of upvest, where she was also leading operations as COO and has been analysing patterns of token sales and token economics.

Among others, at Finleap she has been part of the venture development and launch team of Elinvar, a B2B2C digital asset management platform, where she was managing the initiative to obtain an asset management license at German BaFin.

Since her master studies at ESCP & University of St.Gallen, Romina has been striving to combine a capital market & tech view. She has been observing the value drivers of tech & internet companies in the private and public markets also during her time in equities & derivatives sales & trading at UniCredit and Goldman Sachs. After graduating she has helped to launch a tech & internet equity fund as an equity researcher.
