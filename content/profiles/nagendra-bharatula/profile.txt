Bio:

Dr. Nagendra Bharatula is the Founder and CEO of the G-20 Group, a global investment firm with a focus on asset management, particularly in the areas of fiat and cryptocurrency trading. 

Established in 2010 and headquartered in Zug, Switzerland, the G-20 Group has expanded its operations into various financial services, including foreign exchange, commodity options trading, and market making in both traditional and digital assets.

Dr. Bharatula has a robust academic background with a Ph.D. in Machine Learning and Information Theory from ETH Zurich. Before founding the G-20 Group, he held various quantitative finance roles in several institutional firms, focusing on fixed-income and foreign exchange options trading.
