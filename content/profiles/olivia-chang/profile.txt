Bio: Olivia Chang joined the CNNMoney Switzerland team in 2017 and covers the Swiss business landscape as well as blockchain. Originating from Australia, Olivia began her career in journalism in Sydney where she was Weekend Editor and later Newsroom Producer for Business Insider Australia. She graduated with a Master of Science in Journalism from Columbia University in New York in 2017. She speaks English, German, Cantonese and Mandarin.
