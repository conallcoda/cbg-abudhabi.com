Bio:

Evgeny Xata is a CEO at CyberTrust S.A. , crypto asset management and advisory firm, that specialize in crypto securitization.
Crypto securitization, unlike crypto, provide institutional investors with titled ownership, limited counterparts risks and judicial recovery.
Mr. Xata is actively involved in the development of crypto securitization eco-system, crypto research coverage, trading and asset management. 

Prior to CyberTrust, Evgeny founded and lead the development of the largest Russian digital publishing group, where he currently serves is a Chairman. Prior to that, Evgeny built out and sold investment banking & brokerage operation in Ukraine, where he was activity involved in listing of over 30 companies to PFTS stock exchange, while developing research coverage, brokerage, custody and investment banking infrastructure.   

Prior to that Evgeny Xata, worked at The Boston Consulting Group as a project manager, KPMG Corporate Finance as a Head of Private Equity Advisory Group and Salomon Brother as an intern/analyst.   He graduated from Oxford University.
