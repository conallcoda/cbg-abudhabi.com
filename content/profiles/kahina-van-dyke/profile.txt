Bio:

Kahina is a global financial services & technology leader with a belief in the power of purpose driven teams to drive transformational outcomes in service to clients, communities and businesses in every corner of our world. It takes courageous and creative leadership to unleash breakthrough models that drive more equitable and sustainable prosperity. Kahina has lived in Europe, Middle East, Asia and North America. Her career spans global banking (Citi and Standard Chartered), global payment networks (MasterCard), big technology companies (Facebook) and start-up blockchain companies (Ripple). In each of these roles builds world-class teams places the client and market solutions at the center of the “why” of the change strategy. 

Kahina has a simple philosophy of change - we all have an opportunity to impact the world in many ways. World class outcomes only emerge from world class teams who have the courage to step into uncertainty with humility, integrity and purpose.

A transformational leader who sees change as both normal and necessary to accelerate to the next level of service and impact
