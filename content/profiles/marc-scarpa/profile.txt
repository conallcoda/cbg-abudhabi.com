Bio: Marc Scarpa is a media architect for live, participatory networks. Since the early 90's he has provided leadership on notable and award-winning projects including Global Citizen 2015 Earth Day, Grammy Live, C|NET Television, Microsoft Mixer Studios, You Tube Originals (Red), Arlington Esports Stadium, My Space TV; Woodstock.com; NYC City Hall Blue Room; MTV Interactive and Pseudio Programs Inc. He began this storytelling format journey with 36,000 online and broadcast participants during the 1996 Tibetan Freedom Festival. Live participatory media is now available and enjoyed by billions of people worldwide. Now as co-founder of Decentric Media, its mission is to Broadcast 24/7 live and on-demand finance and tech news to accelerate the adoption of one billion original crypto wallets by 2022.
