Bio: Timo Grossenbacher (1987) does data research, -analysis and -visualization for SRF Data, the data-driven journalism unit of Swiss Public Broadcast (SRF). Before that, he studied Geography and Computer Science at the University of Zurich and worked for the Zurich daily newspaper „Tages-Anzeiger“. He also teaches data journalism at the University of Zurich and is co-organizer of the Zurich chapter of HacksHackers, a get-together of journalists and developers.
