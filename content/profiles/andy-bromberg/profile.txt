Bio: Andy Bromberg is the Co-founder and Special Advisor of CoinList, a platform for deeply vetted tokens to run their token sales, airdrops, and hackathons. CoinList also provides compliance services for many other token sales. Previously, he was the Co-founder and CEO of Sidewire, and before that, he studied Mathematics and Computer Science at Stanford University. While there, he also co-founded the Stanford Bitcoin Group.
