Bio: Pascal Forster (born 1967) has all his life had a true passion for bringing together great minds and ideas, fostering innovation and improving the world. Next to CFC St.Moritz he has helped to set up a number of thriving global communities of business-, thought- and innovation leaders amongst which the Entrepreneurs Roundtable, Club zum Rennweg and World.Minds. As an entrepreneur and investor Pascal is actively involved in building the frameworks for the next stage of digitization and the connected economy. As a private citizen and together with his wife Margarita, in 1999 they founded “Fundacion Avancar” which since actively supports women in Ecuador to become entrepreneurs through a number of education, mentoring and financing programs
