Bio:

Steve Papermaster is the Founder, Chairman and CEO of Nano Vision, dedicated to dramatically accelerating cures to a vast range of cancers, infectious diseases and health conditions. The Nano Cure Platform includes the Nano AI chip, Molecular Data Blockchain, NanoCure Token and therapeutic cure developments with regulatory approvals.

Steve has more than 30 years of experience as an entrepreneur, investor, public policy expert, global speaker and noted author. He is highly experienced in founding, growing, funding, acquiring and investing in innovative technology companies around the world. He has led five of his companies from startup through IPO and billion-dollar-plus revenues and market caps.

Steve served as a direct advisor to President George W. Bush for two terms and co-chaired the President’s Council of Advisors on Science and Technology. Steve also served as a judge for the White House Fellows, one of America’s most prestigious programs for leadership and public service. He actively speaks on the topics of entrepreneurship, technology, science, public policy and China.
