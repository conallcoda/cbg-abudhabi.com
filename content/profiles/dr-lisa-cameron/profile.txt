Bio:

Dr Lisa Cameron was first elected as the Member of Parliament for East Kilbride, Strathaven & Lesmahagow in 2015.  Lisa is the Chair of the All-Party Parliamentary Group on Cryptocurrency and Digital Assets and leads on cryptocurrency work in the House of Commons. She has also been a member of the International Development Committee, Health & Social Care Committee and currently the Ecclesiastical Committee. Lisa is also chair of both the Psychology and Disability All Party Parliamentary Groups in Parliament.  

Prior to becoming a Member of Parliament, Lisa was a clinical psychologist and has over 15 years of experience in Clinical and Forensic Psychology.
